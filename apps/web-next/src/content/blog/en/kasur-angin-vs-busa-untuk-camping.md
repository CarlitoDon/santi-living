Yogyakarta has many beautiful camping spots, from the hills of Mangunan to the beaches of Gunung Kidul. To keep your energy levels up during your adventure, a good night's sleep is still a must.

Many people are torn between bringing an **air bed** or renting a **foam mattress**. Let's compare the two:

## 1. Air Beds
- **Pros**: Very compact when deflated, easy to carry up the mountain.
- **Cons**: Requires a pump, prone to punctures from sharp gravel, and offers less stable back support.

## 2. Foam Mattresses
- **Pros**: Far more comfortable and warm (better thermal insulation from the ground), with no risk of punctures.
- **Cons**: Takes up a lot of space when carried in a car.
