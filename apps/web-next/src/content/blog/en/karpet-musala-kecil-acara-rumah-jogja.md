## How to Prepare a Clean, Small Area

Before the carpets are delivered, prepare the area to prevent them from getting dirty quickly or shifting:

1. Clean the floor of dust, sand, and sharp objects.
2. Ensure the area is not damp or exposed to rain leaks.
3. Separate the footwear area from the carpeted area.
4. Avoid placing food or drinks directly on the carpet.
5. Set up an entry and exit path so guests do not constantly step on the main area.

## Cleaning and Usage Etiquette

Carpets for prayer or communal seating areas need to be kept clean and comfortable. Please let us know in advance if the event involves food, outdoor areas, or potential stains. This information helps our admin team estimate cleaning requirements and logistical risks.

A few simple tips:

- Set up a separate dining area if possible.
- Do not place open drinks in the middle of the carpet.
- Use additional mats or trays for heavy meals.
- Ensure the carpet is not exposed to rainwater or wet ground.
- Coordinate the collection time once the area is cleared.
