One of the common mistakes students make when **renting a mattress in Jogja** is choosing a size that is too large for their limited room space. A cramped room will only disrupt air circulation and your study productivity.

Use the following guide to determine the most suitable mattress size:

## 1. Single Size (90 x 200 cm)
This is the most popular standard size for students.
- **Pros**: Saves a lot of space for a study desk and wardrobe.
- **Best For**: Students living alone with a standard room size (2x3 or 3x3 metres).

## 2. Super Single Size (100 x 200 cm)
Only 10cm wider, but it feels much more spacious when you sleep. The best choice if you want more room to move but still want the room to feel airy.

## 3. Double Size (120 x 200 cm)
Usually known locally as size "No. 3".
- **Caution**: Avoid this size if your room is cramped. This size is ideal if your room is larger than 3.5 x 3.5 metres.
- **Best For**: Those who often study or work on their laptop in bed.

## Tips from Santi Living
Before ordering, measure your room's floor space using a tape measure or the floor tiles as a guide. Make sure there is still enough clearance for the door to open fully.
