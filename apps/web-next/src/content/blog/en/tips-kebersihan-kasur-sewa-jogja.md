# 5 Tips for Keeping a Rented Mattress Clean While Using It in Jogja

Renting a mattress from Santi Living means you get a clean and sanitised unit. However, during the rental period, keeping the mattress clean remains your responsibility. Here are five practical tips you can apply:

## 1. Use a Bed Sheet as a Protective Layer

This is rule number one! Always use a bed sheet as a barrier between your body and the mattress. The sheet will absorb sweat, body oils, and dead skin cells — preventing them from seeping into the mattress. We recommend:
- Change the bed sheets at least **once a week**
- Use breathable cotton bed sheets
- Santi Living also provides **bed sheet rental packages** for customers

## 2. Air the Mattress Regularly

Sunlight is the best natural disinfectant! In Jogja, which is quite often sunny, make the most of this:
- Air the mattress in the sun at least **once a month** for 2-3 hours
- Natural UV rays help kill bacteria and dust mites
- Ensure the mattress is completely dry before using it again

## 3. Vacuum the Mattress Surface

Dust and small particles can accumulate on the surface and in the crevices of the mattress:
- Use a vacuum cleaner with a mattress attachment
- Do this at least **once every two weeks**
- Focus on the seams and corners of the mattress
- Sprinkle a little baking soda before vacuuming to neutralise odours

## 4. Avoid Eating and Drinking on the Bed

Food crumbs and drink spills are the main enemies of mattress hygiene:
- Liquid spills that seep in can cause mould
- Food crumbs attract insects and dust mites
- If you must, use a tray or a sturdy base

## 5. Report Any Issues Immediately

Don't wait! If you find:
- Stubborn stains
- Persistent unpleasant odours
- Signs of damage or mould

## Bonus: Jogja's Humidity

Jogja is quite humid, especially during the rainy season. An extra tip: use a dehumidifier or silica gel around the bed to reduce humidity that could trigger mould on the mattress.

**With this simple care, your rented mattress will remain clean, fresh, and comfortable throughout the rental period.**
