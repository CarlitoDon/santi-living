One of the biggest worries people have when looking for a **mattress rental in Jogja** is cleanliness. *"Was the mattress previously used by someone who was ill?"* or *"Are there bedbugs?"* are completely understandable questions.

At **Santi Living**, we place **Hygiene** as our number one priority, above price and speed. Here is the 7-step sterilisation standard that every single one of our mattresses goes through:

1. **Industrial Vacuuming**: Vacuuming dust and mites right down to the deepest fibres of the foam.
2. **Non-Toxic Liquid Disinfection**: Spraying a germ-killing liquid that is safe for human skin.
3. **UV-C Sterilisation**: Using high-level ultraviolet light to kill microscopic microorganisms.
4. **High-Temperature Bedding Laundry**: All bed sheets are washed in hot water to ensure bacteria are completely eliminated.
5. **Quality Control Check**: A physical inspection to ensure no stains or odours remain.
6. **Sealed Plastic Wrapping**: Mattresses are wrapped in thick plastic immediately after cleaning.
7. **Hygienic Seal**: The plastic is only opened right in front of the customer during handover.

Sleep soundly and peacefully without any worries. Your health is our responsibility.
