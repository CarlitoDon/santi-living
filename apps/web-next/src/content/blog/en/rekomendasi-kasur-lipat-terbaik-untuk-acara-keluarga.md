When extended family from out of town visit for a holiday or a wedding, a house that usually feels spacious can suddenly feel cramped. Sleeping all together in the TV room on a carpet is certainly fun, but it often leaves everyone with aches and pains the next morning.

To solve this, a **folding foam mattress** becomes the unsung hero for the host. 

## Benefits of Using a Folding Mattress for Family Events

- **Outstanding Space Flexibility**: Folding mattresses (available in 2-fold, 3-fold, or 4-fold designs) offer complete freedom in arranging the room. During the day, the mattresses can be neatly stacked in a corner, leaving the living room free for socialising. At night, they can simply be laid out to fit the shape of the room.
- **Extra Comfort**: Despite being highly practical, folding mattresses made from premium/High-Density (HD) foam offer outstanding softness and support. This is perfect for children or elderly relatives to sleep on.
- **Easy to Move**: Their lightweight design means moving the mattresses from an upstairs bedroom to the ground floor is no trouble at all.

## Which is Better: A Standard Foam Mattress or a Folding One?
The answer depends on your needs. 
If you need to sleep on the mattress for a full week and don't need to pack it away every morning, then a standard single-piece foam mattress is best, as there are no gaps between the panels. 

However, for dynamic events where you need to clear the space during the day, the **folding mattress** wins hands down!
