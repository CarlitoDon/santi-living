## Tips for Choosing a Rental Mattress for Your Room

- **Choose the Right Size**: For a single room, a *Single Standard (90x200 cm)* or *Single Super (100x200 cm)* size is more than enough. Avoid renting a Double (120x200) mattress if your room is small, as it will take up too much floor space.

- **Choose a Quality Foam Mattress**: Make sure you rent a foam mattress with a thickness of at least 15 cm. A mattress that is too thin will leave your body aching after a long day.
