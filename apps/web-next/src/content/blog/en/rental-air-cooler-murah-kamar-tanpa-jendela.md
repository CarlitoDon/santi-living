Finding a cheap and strategic boarding room (*kos*) in the centre of Yogyakarta or near busy campus areas (such as Pogung or Babarsari) often requires students to "compromise" on the physical layout of the building. One of the most common compromises is renting a boarding room located on the inside of the building that **has no window** (no access for outdoor air circulation).

Windowless boarding rooms are highly prone to becoming damp, smelling stuffy, and feeling incredibly hot because the air only circulates within the room. Turning on a regular fan in a room like this is like being inside a giant *hairdryer*.

Is there a solution without having to move out? Yes, an **Air Cooler Rental** is your lifesaver.

## Why is an Air Cooler Suitable for Rooms Without Ventilation?

A regular fan fails to cool a windowless room because it doesn't lower the room temperature; it merely speeds up the movement of the existing hot air. 

Air coolers work with a very different mechanism:

1.  **Active Cooling**: Air coolers from **Santi Living** have a water tank that we recommend filling with cold water and *Ice Packs*. When the hot room air is drawn into the machine, it passes through this ice-water pad, so the temperature of the air blown out **actually drops (becomes cooler)**, rather than just being blown around.
2.  **Filtering Fine Dust**: The evaporative process in an air cooler indirectly helps filter dust particles floating around in your closed room, making the air you breathe feel cleaner and fresher (not stuffy).
3.  **Doesn't Make the Room Stuffy Like a Portable AC**: Portable air conditioners **must** have a window to vent the exhaust air. If forced into a windowless room, the room will actually get hotter. An air cooler has no hot exhaust air at all. Extremely safe!
4.  **Student-Friendly Rental Prices**: We offer very cheap monthly air cooler rental rates, far more affordable than worrying about monthly AC electricity bills (an air cooler only uses around 60 Watts of power).
