## When Do Event Crews Need Mattresses?

**Multi-day events.** Exhibitions, bazaars, campus festivals, or multi-day training sessions often require the crew to stay overnight near the venue.

**Events with long setup times.** If setup starts the night before, crew mattresses can help the team take turns resting without having to travel too far home.

**Wedding preparation.** Family, committees, and vendors are sometimes on standby from the day before (D-1) until the day of the event. Extra mattresses reduce the need to buy or borrow mattresses at the last minute.

## Information That Determines the Number of Mattresses

To avoid overestimating, calculate your needs based on the number of people sleeping at the same time. If there are 30 committee members but only 8 are resting in shifts, the number of mattresses can be adjusted. Conversely, if the entire crew is staying overnight at one location, more mattresses may be needed.

Send the following details to the admin:

- The number of crew members who need to sleep at the same time.
- Whether the mattresses will be used in shifts or for full overnight stays.
- The date and time the mattresses must be ready.
- The full location, floor, parking access, and drop-off point.
- The duration of use and collection time.
- Whether pillows, bolsters, blankets, fans, or air coolers are required.

## Tips for a Smooth Drop-Off

Firstly, designate one person to be in charge of the equipment. Having different crew members involved often leads to fragmented communication. Having a single PIC (Person in Charge) will make coordinating delivery, placement, and collection much easier.

Secondly, prepare an area that is safe from rain, catering, production cables, and guest foot traffic. Mattresses should not be placed in spots where people might trip or where they could be damaged by heavy items.

Thirdly, label the zones if it is a large event. For example, "crew rest area", "committee room", or "family room". This helps the delivery team place the items exactly where they are needed.
