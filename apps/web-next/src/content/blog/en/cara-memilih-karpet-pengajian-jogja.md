Choosing a carpet for a pengajian is not just a matter of asking, "How many metres of carpet do you have?". A pengajian gathering usually requires a comfortable floor-seating arrangement that looks neat in photos, is easy to clean, and accommodates the number of guests. Therefore, the **way to choose a carpet for a pengajian** should start with the room layout.

For rug requirements, first visit the [Jogja rug rental](https://permadani.santiliving.com/sewa-karpet-permadani-jogja) page. If the event is a pengajian or tahlilan, you can also read the [Jogja pengajian and tahlilan carpet rental guide](/artikel/sewa-karpet-pengajian-tahlilan-jogja).

## 1. Calculate the Seating Area, Not Just the Number of Guests

The number of guests helps, but the floor area is more important. Send the length and width of the area that will be used for seating. If you do not know the measurements yet, take photos of the room from two angles and provide an estimated number of guests.

For a family pengajian, leave space for walkways, catering tables, the sound system, and an area for the elderly. Avoid covering the entire floor with carpet if it makes it difficult for guests to move around.

## 2. Choose a Pattern that Matches the Atmosphere

Rugs are usually suitable for pengajian, tahlilan, syukuran (thanksgiving) gatherings, and family events as they feel warmer. Specific patterns can be requested, but do not assume all patterns are always available. The admin will check the available options based on the event date.

## 3. Pay Attention to Cleanliness and Stain Risks

Pengajian gatherings often involve food and drinks, children, and guest foot traffic. Ask about the cleaning process, usage rules, and whether there is a deposit or specific terms regarding stain risks. The final policy must be confirmed directly, not assumed from the article.

## 4. Send Complete Details to the Admin

A helpful format to use:

- Date and time of the event.
- Full address and landmarks.
- Number of guests or room size.
- Photos of the area where the carpet will be laid.
- Indoor or semi-outdoor.
- Delivery and collection times.
- Additional requirements: guest mattresses, cushions, fans, or air coolers.

## Common Mistakes

Organisers often book too close to the event date, fail to measure the room, or forget to mention vehicle access. As a result, estimates may change once the location is actually surveyed. It is safer to send complete details right from the start.

## FAQ

**Does a pengajian carpet have to be a rug?**
Not necessarily, but rugs often feel more suitable for floor-seating and family events. Other types can be requested depending on the layout.

**Can it be installed on a terrace?**
This can be discussed. Semi-outdoor areas need to be checked for rain, dust, and cleaning risks.

**Is a deposit required?**
Deposit or cleaning policies must be confirmed via WhatsApp, especially if there is a high risk of stains.
