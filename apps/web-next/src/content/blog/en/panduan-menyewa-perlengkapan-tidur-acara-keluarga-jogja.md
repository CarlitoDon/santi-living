# Complete Guide to Renting Bedding for Large Family Events in Jogja

Hosting a large family event such as a celebration, thanksgiving ceremony, or reunion in Jogja often presents logistical challenges, especially if guests are coming from out of town and need a place to sleep. Renting bedding is a practical solution that is much more cost-effective and convenient than booking extra hotel rooms for every guest.

At Santi Living, we have experience catering to various family events in Jogja with clean, comfortable, and well-maintained bedding. Here is a complete guide for you.

## 1. Calculate Your Needs Accurately

Before renting, calculate exactly how many people will need extra beds. Do not forget to:
- Separate adults and children (children might only need a floor mat or an extra mattress)
- Account for guests arriving with partners
- Add 10-15% as a buffer for unexpected guests or last-minute changes of plan

## 2. Choose the Right Type of Equipment for the Event

### Standard Mattresses
The most common choice for adults. Available in various sizes (Single, Double, Queen, King) to suit your space and budget requirements.

### Extra Mattresses for Children
For children, you can choose:
- Small floor mattresses
- Extra mattresses placed on the floor with blankets and pillows
- Folding mattresses that are practical to store when not in use

### Complete with Supporting Accessories
Do not forget to also rent:
- Blankets (match the number of mattresses)
- Pillows (at least 1 pillow per person, more if possible)
- Mattress protectors (for maximum hygiene)
- Extra bolsters for back pain or simply for comfort

## 3. Consider the Rental Duration

Clearly determine how long you will need the bedding:
- **Daily Rental**: Ideal for events lasting only 1 day
- **Multi-day Rental**: Suitable for 2-3 day events
- **Monthly Rental**: If there are consecutive events or guests staying for a long time

At Santi Living, we offer special discounts for long-term rentals, so the longer you rent, the more cost-effective the daily rate becomes.

## 4. Take Advantage of Delivery, Collection, and Setup Services

One of the biggest advantages of renting from Santi Living is our delivery and collection service across the Jogja area. Our team will:
- Deliver the equipment to your event venue at the scheduled time
- Assist with setup and arrangement if required
- Collect the equipment once the event is over

This removes the logistical burden of transporting heavy items like mattresses to and from the event venue.

## 5. Ensure Maximum Cleanliness and Sanitisation

For large family events where many different people will use the bedding, cleanliness is the top priority. At Santi Living:
- Every mattress unit is professionally cleaned and sanitised between rentals
- Sheets, blankets, and pillows are washed with antibacterial detergent and ironed at high temperatures
- We use safe cleaning products that do not leave harmful chemical residues
- Prior to delivery, we perform a final check to ensure everything is in pristine condition

## 6. Smart Budgeting

Renting bedding is usually much more cost-effective than:
- Booking extra hotel rooms for every guest coming from out of town
- Buying new mattresses just for one event (and then having to find storage space)
- Relying on uncomfortable floor mats or floor mattresses

To get the best price:
- Book at least 3-5 days in advance to ensure availability
- Ask about special packages for large events (mattress + blanket + pillow in one package)
- Consider a multi-day rental if the event lasts more than 1 day to get a better daily rate

## 7. Space Planning and Event Logistics Tips

### Venue Preparation
Ensure there is enough space to place the mattresses comfortably, including room to walk around them. Avoid placing mattresses near doors or busy walkways.

### Clear Labelling
If you are renting different types of mattresses (for adults, children, etc.), consider using simple labels so your team or guests know which is which.

### Delivery and Collection Schedule
Coordinate delivery and collection times clearly so as not to disrupt the flow of the event. Typically, we can deliver in the morning before the event starts and collect in the evening after it finishes.
