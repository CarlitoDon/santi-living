## Why Standing Fans Are Better Than Misting Fans (Misty Fans)?

For semi-indoor events (inside the house or under a canopy tent), **Santi Living** highly recommends using a 16-inch *Stand Fan* (Standing Fan). Here is why:

1.  **Floors Stay Dry and Safe**: *Misty fans* (fans that spray water) are very dangerous when used indoors. Ceramic or marble floors will become extremely slippery and pose a hazard to guests, especially children and the elderly. A *Stand Fan* cools your guests without the risk of slipping.
2.  **Even Circulation**: *Stand Fans* feature an oscillation function (turning left and right), ensuring a fresh breeze is distributed evenly throughout the room, rather than focusing on just one spot.
3.  **Quiet Operation**: If you are inviting a speaker or holding a solemnisation/blessing ceremony, our *Stand Fans* operate with a motor that is much quieter than large industrial blowers.
