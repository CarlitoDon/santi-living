# 5 Tips to Keep Your Rented Mattress Clean During Use

Santi Living rental mattresses are delivered clean and sterilised. However, you can also help maintain their cleanliness.

1. Use bedsheets and a mattress protector — change them at least once a week
2. Air it out regularly — morning sunlight kills bacteria
3. Vacuum the mattress every two weeks — use a HEPA filter for dust and mites
4. Avoid eating and drinking in bed — prevent spills and mould
5. Report any stains or odours immediately — we are ready to help with a solution

With simple care, your rented mattress will remain comfortable throughout the contract.
