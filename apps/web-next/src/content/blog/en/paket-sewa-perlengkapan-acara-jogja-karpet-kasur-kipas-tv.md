## How to Put Together a Package Without Going Over Budget

First, separate the must-have items from the optional extras. For overnight events, mattresses and bedding are usually a higher priority than decoration. For booths, display TVs and cooling systems might be more important than mattresses. For religious gatherings (pengajian) or floor-seating events, floor coverings are the priority.

Second, determine quantities based on zones, not just the number of guests. Organisers can divide their needs into registration zones, crew rest zones, guest zones, dining zones, and family zones. This approach makes estimation much tidier as every item has a clear function.

Third, send photos or layout sketches if available. Santi Living does not need complex technical drawings; photos of the location, estimated area sizes, and power points are enough to help our admin give initial guidance.
