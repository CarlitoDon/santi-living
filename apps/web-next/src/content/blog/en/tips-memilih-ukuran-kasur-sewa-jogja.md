# Tips for Choosing the Right Rental Mattress Size

Choosing the wrong mattress size can lead to an uncomfortable night's sleep. Here is a guide to choosing the right size for your needs.

## Standard Sizes

- **Single (90x200)** — for one adult. Suitable for bedsits, small bedrooms
- **Double (120x200)** — more spacious for one person. Perfect for small rented homes
- **Queen (160x200)** — standard for couples. Comfortable for two people
- **King (180x200)** — luxurious and spacious. For large bedrooms or families with small children

## How to Choose

1. Measure the room size — leave at least 60cm of space around the mattress to walk comfortably
2. Consider the number of users — single for one person, queen/king for two
3. Height — add 20cm to your height for the mattress length
4. Access — make sure the mattress can fit through doors and stairwells (especially for first-floor bedsits)
