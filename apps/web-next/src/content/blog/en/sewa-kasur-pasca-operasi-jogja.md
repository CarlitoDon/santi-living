## Why is an Orthopaedic Mattress Important Post-Surgery?

After surgery, the body needs extra support that a regular mattress cannot provide:

- **Even pressure distribution** — reducing pressure points on the surgical wound area, preventing additional pain
- **Spinal alignment support** — keeping the spine aligned, preventing back pain caused by incorrect sleeping positions
- **The right firmness** — not too firm (which can cause pain and pressure sores) or too soft (making it difficult for patients to move and get up)
- **Ease of mobility** — patients can get out of bed more easily without excessive assistance
