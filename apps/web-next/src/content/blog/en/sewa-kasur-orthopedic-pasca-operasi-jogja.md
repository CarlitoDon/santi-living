# Orthopaedic Mattress Rental for Post-Surgery Recovery in Jogja

Post-surgery recovery requires maximum rest. The right mattress can speed up healing.

## Why an Orthopaedic Mattress?

Orthopaedic mattresses are specially designed to:
- Distribute body pressure evenly
- Keep the spine aligned
- Reduce pressure on the surgical area
- Prevent bedsores (pressure ulcers)

## Benefits of Renting for Recovery

Recovery usually only takes 2–8 weeks. Buying an orthopaedic mattress costing millions for short-term use is inefficient. Renting gives you:
- Hospital-quality mattress without the hospital price tag
- Can be returned once recovery is complete
- Free delivery and collection — no need to worry about lifting heavy mattresses
- Professional sterilisation before delivery

## Tips for Choosing a Post-Surgery Mattress

1. Consult your doctor about the required level of firmness
2. Choose a mattress with a waterproof cover (making it easier to clean)
3. Ensure it is wide enough to move comfortably
4. Consider adding a wedge pillow or support cushion
