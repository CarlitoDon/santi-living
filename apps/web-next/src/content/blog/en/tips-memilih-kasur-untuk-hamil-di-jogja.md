# Tips for Choosing a Mattress for Pregnant Women in Jogja: Comfortable and Safe

Pregnancy is a period that requires optimal rest and sleep quality. However, many pregnant women experience difficulty sleeping comfortably due to physical and hormonal changes. Choosing the right mattress can help reduce complaints such as back pain, pressure on joints, and difficulty changing positions.

At Santi Living, we understand the special needs of pregnant women regarding their sleeping environment. Here is a practical guide to choosing the right rental mattress in Jogja.

## 1. Prioritise Good Back Support

Pregnant women often experience lower back pain due to the baby's increasing weight. Choose a mattress that provides sufficient lumbar support without being too firm. A medium-firm mattress is often the ideal choice as it provides enough support while still contouring to the body.

## 2. Pay Attention to Pressure Relief

In addition to support, the mattress must be able to distribute body weight evenly to reduce pressure on areas such as the shoulders, hips, and knees. Memory foam or hybrid mattresses with a responsive foam layer can be a good choice for this.

## 3. Choose a Size with Enough Room to Move

As the pregnancy progresses, pregnant women need more space to change positions and stretch their legs. Consider renting a Queen or King-size mattress if your budget allows, or at least ensure a Full-size mattress has enough length to stretch out without feeling restricted.

## 4. Check Resistance to Heat and Humidity

Hormonal changes during pregnancy can cause pregnant women to sweat more easily. Choose a mattress with breathable materials such as gel-infused foam, natural latex, or a pocket spring mattress with a good air circulation system. This will help keep your body temperature comfortable throughout the night.

## 5. Ensure Maximum Cleanliness and Sterilisation

Cleanliness is a crucial aspect, especially for pregnant women whose immune systems may be more sensitive. At Santi Living, all our mattress units undergo a sterilisation and deep cleaning process between rentals. You can also request fresh bed sheets and blankets every time you rent a mattress.

## 6. Test the Mattress in Person if Possible

If possible, take the opportunity to lie down on the mattress for a moment before renting it. This will give you a direct idea of the level of comfort and support provided. Pay attention to how the mattress contours when you lie on your side (the recommended sleeping position for pregnant women).

## Conclusion

Choosing the right mattress during pregnancy is not just a matter of comfort, but also of your health and that of your baby. By paying attention to factors such as back support, pressure relief, size, temperature regulation, and cleanliness, you can achieve good quality sleep throughout your pregnancy.
