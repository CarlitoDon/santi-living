**Folding mattress or foam mattress?** This question often arises when people want to rent an extra mattress. Each has its own advantages, depending on your needs.

## Comparison: Folding Mattress vs Foam Mattress

| Aspect | Folding Mattress | Foam Mattress |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| **Comfort** | Sufficient for temporary sleep | More comfortable for daily sleep |
| **Thickness** | 5-8 cm | 10-20 cm |
| **Storage** | Easy to fold, space-saving | Cannot be folded |
| **Durability** | Less suitable for long-term use | More durable |
| **Rental Price** | Cheaper | Slightly more expensive |
| **Best Suited For** | Unexpected guests, indoor camping | Student accommodation, recovery, long-term rental |

## When Should You Choose a Folding Mattress?

✅ Unexpected guests arrive and need a quick sleeping arrangement
✅ Space is very limited, the mattress needs to be stored away after use
✅ Temporary needs (1-3 days)
✅ Indoor camping or retreat events

## When Should You Choose a Foam Mattress?

✅ Long-term rental (weekly/monthly)
✅ Need maximum comfort for sleeping every night
✅ Recovering patients or the elderly
✅ Students who need a mattress for the new term
