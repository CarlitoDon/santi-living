# Renting vs Buying a Mattress in Jogja: Cost Comparison

The dilemma when moving to Jogja: should you rent or buy a mattress?

## Cost Comparison

Renting: Rp 150k–300k per month. 3-year cost: Rp 5.4m–10.8m. Free delivery and collection. No maintenance worries.

Buying: Rp 1m–3m upfront. 3-year cost: purchase price + maintenance + moving costs of Rp 200k–500k. Resale value is only 20–40%.

## When is Renting More Suitable?

- Students or contract workers (1–3 years)
- Living in a boarding house (kost) or rented house
- Frequently relocating
- Need the flexibility to change mattress types

## When is Buying More Suitable?

- Permanent home (>5 years)
- Very specific preferences
- Budget for a long-term investment
