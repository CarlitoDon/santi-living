## Santi Living Mattress Specifications

### Material: High Density Foam (HD Foam)

- **Density:** 20-24 kg/m³ — star-rated hotel mattress standard
- **Thickness:** 15-20 cm (depending on size)
- **Cover:** Soft knit fabric, anti-allergenic, easy to wash
- **Durability:** Does not sag easily even after years of use

Why HD Foam and not a spring bed?
- **More hygienic** — No gaps for dust and dust mites
- **More durable** — No springs that can break or rust
- **Lighter** — Easy to move and deliver
- **More affordable rental price** — Lower maintenance costs

## Why is HD Foam Suitable for Rental?

| Criteria | HD Foam | Spring Bed |
|----------|---------|------------|
| Cleanliness | ✅ Easy to clean thoroughly | ❌ Spring gaps = dust nest |
| Durability | ✅ 5-7 years | ❌ 3-5 years |
| Weight | ✅ Light, easy to move | ❌ Heavy, difficult to deliver |
| Comfort | ✅ Even back support | ⚠️ Pressure points on springs |

## Maintenance After Rental

Every returned mattress undergoes:
1. **Visual inspection** — Check for damage, stains, odours
2. **Vacuum deep clean** — Dust and dirt are completely removed
3. **Spot treatment** — Stains are removed with a special cleaner
4. **UV-C sanitisation** — Bacteria and viruses are killed with UV light
5. **Anti-bacterial spray** — Additional protective layer
6. **Drying** — Mattress is dried completely
7. **Quality check** — Final inspection before being stored for the next renter
