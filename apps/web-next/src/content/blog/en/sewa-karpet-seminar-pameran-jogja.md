## Seminar Areas That Often Require Carpets

### Speaker Stages

For seminars, talk shows, campus workshops, or training sessions, carpets can be used in the speaker area to make the small stage look more professional. Send the stage dimensions, stage height, and a photo of the front side so our admin team can estimate your requirements more accurately.

### Registration and Guest Pathways

The registration pathway is often the first point attendees see. Carpeting makes this area look more organised, especially when the registration desk is located in a lobby, campus hall, small hotel, or multi-purpose building.

### Aisles and Photo Spot Backdrops

For events with heavy media coverage or photography, carpets can help define the aisle leading to the stage or the group photo area. ## Exhibition and Booth Areas That Often Require Carpets

### Exhibition Booths

Booth carpets help distinguish exhibitor areas, create a tidier look, and provide a more comfortable experience for booth staff. Don't just state the number of booths; send the area layout, the size of each booth, and whether the carpet is needed for the entire space or just a portion of it.

### Visitor Pathways

For expos, bazaars, or campus exhibitions, carpets can be used along the main pathways to make the visitor flow clearer. If the area is expected to be busy, let us know the event duration and the risk of food/drink stains so we can provide a more accurate cleaning estimate.

## Size Checklist for Booths and Stages

Before requesting an estimate, please prepare:

- The booth plan or area layout.
- The length x width dimensions of each zone.
- The number of event days and the setup schedule.
- The venue location and loading access.
- Whether the carpet will be used indoors, semi-outdoors, or outdoors.
- Your preferred colour, if any.
- Additional equipment: TV displays, fans, air coolers, organiser mattresses, pillows, or blankets.

This checklist is important because the requirements for **booth carpet rental in Jogja** differ from **stage carpet rental in Jogja**. Booths usually focus on the display area size, while stages focus on the front appearance, speaker footing, and photography/videography.
