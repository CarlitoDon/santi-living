## Don't Just Choose the Colour, Check the Area's Function Too

Gold rugs can look great, but the function of the area remains important. Seating areas for prayer gatherings require different comfort levels and cleaning compared to family photo areas. A covered terrace requires more attention than an indoor living room.

Before ordering, determine whether the rug will be used for:

- Long periods of sitting during the main event.
- Guest transit areas before entering the house.
- Children's or elderly areas.
- Decoration corners that won't be stepped on much.
- Floor-dining areas with a risk of stains.
