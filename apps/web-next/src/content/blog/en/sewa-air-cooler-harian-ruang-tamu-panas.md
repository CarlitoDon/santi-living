As Indonesians who love socialising, our homes often become gathering places for extended family, neighbourhood association meetings, or community gatherings. The problem is, when there are more than 10 people in the living room or family room, the temperature rises drastically. A room that was initially cool will turn stuffy and hot.

Turning on all the fans in the house is often not enough because they only circulate the hot air. If you don't have air conditioning in your living room, **renting a daily Air Cooler** is a lifesaver in front of your guests.

## Why an Air Cooler is Perfect for Home Events?

1.  **Cools, Doesn't Just Blow**: The evaporative cooling technology of the Air Cooler (supported by our *Blue Ice Packs*) will significantly lower the surrounding air temperature. The breeze feels cool and refreshing.
2.  **No Need to Drill Through Walls**: If you buy or rent a portable AC, you still have to figure out how to route the hot air exhaust hose outside. Air Coolers are free from those troublesome hoses. Simply plug it in, and the cool air will spread.
3.  **Elegant Appearance**: The design of the Air Cooler units we rent out looks modern and minimalist. It won't ruin the aesthetics of your living room when taking photos with your guests.
4.  **More Cost-Effective than Renting a Misty Fan (Mist Fan)**: For indoor events, mist fans are not recommended as they make the floor wet and can damage electronics. An Air Cooler is the best indoor option and is much cheaper.

Your guests will appreciate your extra effort in providing comfort. Be the perfect host without having to invest in buying a new AC for the living room.
