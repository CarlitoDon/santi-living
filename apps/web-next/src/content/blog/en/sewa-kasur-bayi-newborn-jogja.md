# Baby Mattress and Bedding Hire for New Families in Jogja

Welcoming a new family member requires a lot of preparation, including bedding.

## Why Hire?

Babies grow quickly. A purchased baby mattress may no longer fit within 6–12 months. Hiring is more cost-effective because:
- No need to buy equipment that is quickly outgrown
- You can change sizes as they grow
- Hygiene is guaranteed — each unit is sterilised

## Tips for Choosing

1. Flat and firm surface (SIDS safety)
2. The right size to fit the cot
3. Waterproof, easily removable cover
4. Free from harmful chemicals
