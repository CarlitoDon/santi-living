"What is the actual standard price for renting a mattress in the Yogyakarta area?"

This question is most frequently asked by newcomers, new university students, and event organisers. Thanks to healthy competition, the cost of renting home furniture—including mattresses—in Yogyakarta is still very budget-friendly.

To help you plan your budget, we have summarised the average price list.

## Estimated Mattress Rental Prices by Type

### 1. "Mattress Only" Option
The most popular option because it is the cheapest. You do not get pillows or bedsheets. Ideal if you have your own bedding.
- **Single Mattress (90x200):** Rp20,000 – Rp30,000 / day
- **Double Mattress (120x200):** Rp35,000 – Rp45,000 / day
- **Weekly Rental:** Rp150,000 – Rp250,000
- **Monthly Rental:** From Rp300,000 / month

### 2. "Complete Package" Option (Mattress + Bedsheet + Pillow + Blanket)
Highly recommended for hotel/villa guests, or if you have just arrived in Jogja. You can just *lie back and relax* as soon as you arrive!
- **Single Package (1 Person):** From Rp35,000 / day
- **Queen/King Package (2-3 People):** Rp55,000 – Rp65,000 / day

*Note: There is usually an optional extra charge if you need a bolster pillow or an extra blanket for cold nights.*

## Are There Any Hidden Costs? (Delivery Fees)
One thing you must watch out for when renting from roadside rental shops is the transparency of **delivery and collection fees**. 

Tip: Ask before you rent!
- Is the price "all-in"?
- How much is the delivery rate per kilometre?
