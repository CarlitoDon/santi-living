# Mattress Hire for the Elderly: Tips for Choosing a Safe and Comfortable One in Jogja

Older people or the elderly need a different kind of mattress. It is not just about being soft, but it must be safe and supportive.

## Special Needs of the Elderly

- Easy to get on and off — the right mattress height (not too low)
- Spinal support — memory foam or latex
- Not too soft — makes it difficult to get up
- Anti-slip — prevents the mattress from shifting

## Recommended Features

1. Firm mattress edges for support when sitting
2. Breathable cover — prevents overheating
3. Hypoallergenic material — for sensitive skin
4. Motion isolation — if sharing the bed with a partner

## Why Hire?

- You can try it out first before deciding on the right type
- If health conditions change, it is easy to switch types
- No need for a large investment in later life
