## Tips for Arranging the Living Room and Terrace

To make your family event more comfortable, try to prepare the following before the carpets are delivered:

- Clean the floor of dust, sand, or sharp objects.
- Move small tables that obstruct guest walkways.
- Set up a footwear area so the carpets do not get dirty quickly.
- Separate the dining area and seating area if possible.
- Ensure the terrace area is protected from rain and dampness.
