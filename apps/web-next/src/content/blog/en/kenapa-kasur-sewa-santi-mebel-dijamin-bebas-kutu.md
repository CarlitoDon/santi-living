There is a latent (hidden) fear often felt by consumers who have never rented fabric items before: *"Is it safe? Is it dirty? Will there be mites or bed bugs passed on from the previous user?"*

This concern is 100% valid! A damp, second-hand mattress from a stranger is the perfect breeding ground for these itchy parasites. Having itchy skin at night can easily ruin the joy of your holiday or your stay in a rented room.

However, you can cast those worries aside if you rent from a trusted rental service. Here at Santi Mebel, we treat hygiene not as an *optional extra*, but as an absolute **Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)**.

## Mattress Cleaning Workflow

Every physical mattress returned to our laundry/storage facility after being collected from a previous customer is never immediately rented out to the next customer the following day. Here is our process:

**1. UV Sun Drying**
Foam mattresses are aired out and rotated in the yard, exposed to bright ultraviolet sunlight. Sunlight is highly effective at eliminating moisture, microscopic mould, and pesky fungi.

**2. Deep-Vacuuming**
Using a vacuum cleaner equipped with heavy-duty mite-suction technology. All lint, pollen, dead skin cells, micro-parasites, and even kapok dust are completely sucked away from deep within the foam's pores!

**3. Cover & Bed Sheet Laundry (Hot Wash)**
All outer fabrics, package sheets, and pillow covers are stripped off immediately and sent to the laundry facility. They are washed using a fabric-specific antibacterial detergent at a warm temperature to comprehensively destroy any bed bug eggs.

**4. Disinfectant Spray & Scented Extract**
Once clean, white, and dried, the mattress is sprayed with a hotel-grade germ-killing disinfectant mist, finished with a touch of fresh laundry fragrance. You won't be sleeping with someone else's sweat odour, but rather the scent of chamomile or lavender.

**5. Sealed Plastic Wrapping**
Our clients' favourite part: the mattress is delivered tightly <em>wrapped</em> in a large, secure plastic seal to ensure no mud, road dust, or rain splashes from the courier's car or motorbike get onto it. The plastic is only ripped open at your location!
