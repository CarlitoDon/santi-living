## Why Are Carpet Rental Prices Not Always the Same?

Event carpet prices are influenced by several key factors:

- **Carpet type**: red carpet, rugs, booth carpet, or floor seating mats.
- **Area size**: length x width, stage area, entrance pathway, or number of guests.
- **Rental duration**: one-day event, multiple days, or setup the day before.
- **Event location**: Sleman, Jogja City, Bantul, Godean, Gamping, Depok, or other areas.
- **Logistics**: delivery time, collection time, vehicle access, stairs, and distance from the workshop.
- **Cleaning and stain risks**: outdoor events, food, rain, or high-traffic areas.
- **Deposit or damage fee**: can be discussed if the risk of damage or staining is significant.
