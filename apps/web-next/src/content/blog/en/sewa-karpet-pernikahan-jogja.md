## Wedding Areas That Usually Require Carpets

### Guest Entrances

A red carpet or runner makes the entrance look tidier, especially for homes, small venues, homestays, or outdoor areas where you want to add a formal touch. The term **event carpet runner** usually refers to a long carpet for the processional pathway, rather than a floor-seating rug.

### Akad Area or Small Stage

If the akad is held at home, in a pendopo (pavilion), or the living room, carpets help define the main area and make photo documentation look cleaner. Send the stage dimensions, pathway length, and photos of the area so the admin can check feasible options.

### Family Seating Area

For large families waiting before the event, rugs or floor mats can make the seating area more comfortable. This requirement is different from a red carpet: rugs focus more on seating comfort, while red carpets focus more on pathways and a ceremonial feel.

## Red Carpet or Rug?

Choose a **red carpet** if the primary need is for a formal pathway, VIP area, akad, stage, or grand entrance. Choose a **rug** if the primary need is for floor seating, the family living room, pre-wedding prayer gatherings (pengajian), or a more relaxed waiting area.
