## Tips for Arranging Wedding Guest Accommodation

### 1. Calculate the Number of Guests Well in Advance
Make a list of guests who are confirmed to need accommodation. Add a 20% buffer for unexpected guests.

### 2. Prepare the Rooms
The living room, lounge, or even the terrace can be turned into sleeping areas. Ensure there is adequate ventilation.

### 3. Separate Rooms by Gender
Provide separate rooms for male and female guests for mutual comfort.

### 4. Provide Additional Equipment
Blankets for those who are cold, fans for those who are hot. Santi Living provides all of these.
