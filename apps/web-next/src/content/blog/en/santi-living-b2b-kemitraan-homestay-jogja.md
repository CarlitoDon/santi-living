Santi Living, the trusted daily mattress rental division established by **Santi Mebel**, has officially announced its strategic expansion into the B2B (Business-to-Business) market in Yogyakarta. This move is focused on helping managers of homestays, guest houses, and short-term accommodation in the Yogyakarta, Sleman, Bantul, and Kulonprogo areas manage occupancy surges and accommodate large family groups without having to invest in new mattresses themselves.

As part of this commitment, Santi Living has mapped and curated **40 top homestay prospects** in Yogyakarta with a total capacity of up to **396 pax** ready to become strategic partners. This partnership comes with various extra accommodation models that are highly profitable for lodging owners.

---

### Three Strategic Pillars of Santi Living's B2B Partnership

To support the operational productivity of short-term property managers, Santi Living offers 3 flexible collaboration schemes:

1. **Referral Partnership Programme (Flat 10% Net Commission)**
   Homestay owners or staff simply need to recommend hygienic extra bed options from Santi Living when guests make a booking. Payment and delivery are handled directly by Santi Living. Once the transaction is successful, the partner receives a flat 10% net referral commission per mattress.
   
2. **Seasonal Bulk Hire (B2B Bulk Discount)**
   During national holidays, Eid, New Year, or graduation ceremonies at major universities in Jogja (such as UGM, UNY, UMY, UAD, UII), homestays often experience overcapacity. Santi Living provides bulk hire packages (5 to 15 mattresses at once) with significant wholesale discounts to help accommodate group bookings.

3. **Consignment Stocking Drop-Point**
   For properties with a very high volume of last-minute guests, Santi Living is ready to place a safety stock of 2-3 premium mattresses directly in the homestay's storage room as consignment stock. This allows for instant extra bed availability in less than 30 minutes!

### Service Areas & Strict Policies of Santi Living

We are proud to serve most of the Special Region of Yogyakarta to support the local tourism ecosystem. Our free delivery and collection service reaches all areas in **Sleman, Bantul, Yogyakarta City, and Kulonprogo (Wates)**. 

However, to maintain service quality, the safety of our delivery fleet, and operational mileage feasibility, Santi Living strictly enforces an **out-of-service boundary policy**. We **do not offer** mattress delivery to the **Gunungkidul area (Semanu, Wonosari, etc.)** due to the steep hilly terrain and route conditions that pose a high risk to the safety of our couriers transporting the mattresses.
