# Types of Mattresses Available for Rent at Santi Living Jogja

Santi Living provides various types of rental mattresses. Find the right option for you.

## 1. Standard Foam Mattress
Suitable for students and shared housing. Comfortable, lightweight, and affordable. Thickness: 15-20cm.

## 2. Premium Foam Mattress
Higher density and more durable. Ideal for long-term rentals and families.

## 3. Memory Foam Mattress
Contours to your body shape, reducing pressure points. Ideal for those with back pain or anyone needing extra comfort.

## 4. Orthopaedic Mattress
Designed for spinal health. Firmer and more supportive surface. Suitable for the elderly or medical recovery.

## 5. Folding Mattress
Practical for limited spaces. Can be stored away when not in use. Ideal for unexpected guests.

## 6. Baby & Children's Mattress
Small size, safe materials, and waterproof cover. For newborns to toddlers.

All types are available in various sizes (single, double, queen, king). Contact Santi Living to consult on the best choice for you.
