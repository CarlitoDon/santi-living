Before contacting the vendor, use this **event carpet rental checklist** so the admin team can provide a quicker estimate. Carpets for weddings, prayer gatherings, seminars, exhibitions, and booths cannot be accurately calculated just from the phrase "I need a carpet for an event".

Start with the [Jogja carpet rental](https://karpet.santiliving.com/sewa-karpet-jogja) page, then prepare the details below before messaging on WhatsApp.

## Main Checklist

- **Event type**: wedding, prayer gathering, memorial service, seminar, exhibition, bazaar, booth, or family event.
- **Event date**: including whether the carpet needs to be installed the day before.
- **Full location**: address, landmarks, and service area.
- **Area size**: length x width of guest pathways, rooms, stages, or booths.
- **Venue photos**: send from multiple angles to make the layout easier to understand.
- **Carpet type**: red carpet, rugs, booth carpet, or floor seating mats.
- **Rental duration**: delivery time, installation time, dismantling time, and collection time.
- **Loading access**: parking, stairs, lifts, narrow alleys, or venue rules.
- **Additional requirements**: guest mattresses, pillows, fans, air coolers, TVs, or other equipment.

## Why Must the Size Be Clear?

Size is the biggest factor in determining the estimate. A 2 x 10-metre guest pathway is very different from a 6 x 8-metre floor seating area. If you don't have a tape measure yet, send photos of the area and an estimated size. The admin team can help request any missing details.

## Checklist for Hotel or Building Venues

For hotels, halls, or formal venues, please add the following information:

- Name of the venue PIC (person in charge), if available.
- Loading and unloading time regulations.
- Parking location for delivery vehicles.
- Whether installation requires using a lift or stairs.
- Areas where tape must not be applied or that must not be covered by carpet.

## Checklist for Home Events

For homes, narrow alleys, or pavilions, check:

- Whether the area is indoor, a terrace, or a yard.
- Risk of rain or dust.
- Width of vehicle access.
- Whether food or drinks will be consumed on the carpet.
- What time guests will start arriving.
