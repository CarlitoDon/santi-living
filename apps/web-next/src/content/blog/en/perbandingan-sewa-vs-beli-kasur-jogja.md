## When Does Buying Make More Sense?

Buying a mattress is the right choice if:
- You plan to stay in Jogja for **more than 5 years**
- You have a very specific preference for a particular brand
- You want permanent ownership without depending on a service provider

Renting from Santi Living is a smart choice if:
- You are a **student** only staying in Jogja for a few years
